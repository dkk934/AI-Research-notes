# Contribution Rules for Notes Structure

This document defines the **standard structure** contributors should follow when adding new learning notes to this repository. The goal is to keep all notes **consistent, beginner-friendly, and easy to navigate**, especially for learners starting AI from zero.

---

When adding a new note, **create a new markdown file** and follow the phase-based structure below.

* What is Artificial Intelligence
* Difference between:

  * AI vs Machine Learning vs Deep Learning
* Rule-based AI vs Learning-based AI
* Types of AI:

  * Narrow AI (Weak AI)
  * General AI (AGI)
  * Super AI (Theoretical)

## Outcome

‚úî You understand what AI is and why you are learning it
